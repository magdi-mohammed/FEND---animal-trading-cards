Lion

A lion’s roar can be heard from as far as 5 miles away.

  • Scientific Name: Panthera leo
  • Average Body Length: 1.6–2.5 m.
  • Weight: 150 to 250 kg for males.
  • Habitat: In Africa, lions can be found in savanna grasslands.

The lion has forever been a symbol of strength, power and ferocity.
